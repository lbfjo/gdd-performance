# Staff Control Priority List

## P1 - Immediate Control

1. Manual attendance correction
   - Add a staff-only check-in for an athlete and date.
   - Remove mistaken check-ins for an athlete and date.
   - Show who currently has check-ins on the selected date.

2. Athlete status flags
   - Mark athletes as active, injured, excused, limited, needs follow-up, or away.
   - Store a short staff note with the status.
   - Use status flags to reduce noisy alerts for excused/injured/away athletes.

## P2 - Operational Follow-Up

3. Follow-up queue
   - Surface athletes with no sessions this week, no weight logs, booked but no check-in, or missed nutrition appointments.
   - Group by urgency and reason.

4. Booking control
   - Cancel, move, or create bookings on behalf of athletes.
   - Record whether the action was performed by staff.

## P3 - Individual Planning

5. Athlete-specific weekly targets
   - Override the team weekly target per athlete.
   - Exempt an athlete for a specific week.

6. Staff notes timeline
   - Keep dated staff-only notes per athlete.
   - Categorize notes as attendance, training, nutrition, injury, or admin.

## P4 - Reporting

7. Control exports
   - Export no-shows, attendance corrections, booking mismatches, and nutrition compliance.

8. Weekly report builder
   - Generate a compact weekly summary for coaches with attendance, risk flags, and exceptions.
